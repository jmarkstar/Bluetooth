    Bluetooth UUID Definition name.

    The Bluetooth SIG allocates a 16-bit Universally Unique Identifier (UUID) for use with a custom GATT-based service defined by the member, as well as a 16-bit Universally Unique Identifier (UUID) assignment for Standards Development Organizations (SDOs).
